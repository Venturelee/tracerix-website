[ How It Works ]

FROM DEVICE TO DASHBOARD IN 4 STEPS.

Install once. Monitor forever. Tracerix makes GPS intelligence effortless — no engineers required.

01
Install Device

Plug TRX-UNIT into your vehicle's OBD-II port or hardwire it. Setup takes under 3 minutes — no tools needed.

02
Connect & Transmit

The device locks onto GPS satellites and transmits live location data via NB-IoT / LTE-M with satellite failover.

03
Cloud Processing

Data streams to our AES-256 encrypted cloud. AI analyzes routes, flags anomalies, and generates live alerts automatically.

04
Monitor & Act

Access your full fleet dashboard from any device. Get instant SMS / email / webhook alerts. React to incidents in seconds.
